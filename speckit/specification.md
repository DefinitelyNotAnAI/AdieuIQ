### Solution Overview

- **Goal**: Deliver personalized adoption and upsell recommendations for
  customer support personas in an industry-agnostic manner.

- **Scope**: Azure-native solution integrating Fabric IQ and Foundry IQ
  for semantic intelligence and knowledge retrieval.

### Core Components

- **Fabric IQ**: Semantic modeling and real-time operational
  intelligence.

- **Foundry IQ**: Enterprise-grade knowledge retrieval and grounding.

- **Azure AI Foundry SDK**: Multi-agent orchestration.

- **Power BI**: Visualization layer for insights.

- **GitHub Copilot + SpecKit**: AI-assisted implementation.

### Personas

- **Customer Support Agent**: Needs real-time insights and
  recommendations during interactions.

- **Customer Success Manager**: Requires adoption analytics and upsell
  opportunities.

### Key Features

- Personalized recommendations based on usage, sentiment, and historical
  interactions.

- Real-time dashboard for adoption metrics and upsell triggers.

- Multi-agent orchestration for contextual reasoning and
  decision-making.
